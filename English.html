    Brief Introduction

      Mainly engaged in data (including text, network and civil aviation) analysis and application, civil aviation informatization. He successively visited the Information Network Engineering Research Center of Tsinghua University (2013.09-2014.07) and the National Institute of Communications and Information (I2R) of Singapore (2014.09-2015.02). At present, in cooperation with Nankai University, a National Natural Science Foundation of China project and a key project of the Tianjin Municipal Education Commission's scientific research plan are being researched. As the first author or corresponding author in Chinese first-level journals "Journal of Software", "Journal of Computer", etc., international academic conferences SIGIR, ACL, IJCAI, CIKM, ECML/PKDD, etc., international SCI source journals Applied Soft Computing, Knowledge and Information Systems have published many papers. In addition, in recent years, I have done some very specific and effective work with other teachers in our college in the planning of civil aviation enterprise informatization construction. , "Capital Airport Power Energy Co., Ltd. Operation and Control Center Construction Feasibility Study Plan", "Chongqing Airport Information System Planning" and "Guangzhou Baiyun Airport Co., Ltd. Information System Planning".

    Research topic (last five years)
